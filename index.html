What is ART?

AFH is an organization which is dedicated to promoting all forms of art. AFH provides a platform for artists, art teachers and art patrons around the world to interact exchange ideas and opportunities. AFH is moving forward with the conviction that art has the potential to engrave a positive signature on humanity. We attach weight to the fact that the duty of an artist is to embolden art and its benefits for the revitalization of the society.

Is it painting or singing? Yes, that too but does that mean that if you don't know these stereotypical forms of art you aren't an artist? Well, if you do think so, that's where you are wrong. Art happens to be way more than just that. There is art in every walk of life. There is art in every move you make and therefore it is as right as rain to call you an artist. If you aren't convinced yet look at your dress, Does it match? If it does, there you go that's art. But guess what! you are an artist if it doesn't as well, only a different kind of artist.

We're an NGO

Which is dedicated to Fine Arts, Culture and Humanity, providing a network for artists, art teachers and art patrons around the world.
