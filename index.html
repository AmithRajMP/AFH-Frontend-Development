About Us

We're an NGO

Which is dedicated to Fine Arts, Culture and Humanity, providing a network for artists, art teachers and art patrons around the world.

AFH forum was launched pompously in the year 2014 and has been expanding its community rapidly ever since, as a unit of Ranjini Kalakendra. We at AFH are moving forward with the conviction that art has the potential to engrave a positive signature on mankind. We attach weight to the fact that the duty of an artist is to embolden art and its benefits for the revitalization of the society.
